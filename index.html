Evan's Music Library

The following tracks were made with the intent to be used in my Minecraft mod, Demons and Dreams.

Storm on the Starry Sea

When I started working on this track, I intended it to play in a mysterious region with a dark undertone, but it eventually expanded into its own track that told its own story. It is now a music disc in-game.

Mental Mire

As the shadows darken and form into monsters, you begin to hear the dissonant, warped drone of your own insanity.

Fateful Approach

You've made it to the realm on the edge of reality. The dragon rests ahead. Are you ready to approach it?
