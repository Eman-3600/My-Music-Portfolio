Evan's Scrapped Tracks

The following tracks and track versions have been scrapped.

The Weight of Your Demons v1

This is the original version of "the Weight of Your Demons". It was one of my first ever tracks and didn't quite strike the tones I wanted, so I've decided to remake it. The chorus here is the Angel motif, which I repurposed in the new version.

So the Moon Cried Out

Another one of my first tracks, "So the Moon Cried Out" has a few issues that have led to it being replaced by "Against the Dreadful Night".
